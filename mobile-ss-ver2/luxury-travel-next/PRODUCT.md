# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Affluent travelers, couples, families and private groups researching premium Southeast Asia trips. They read destination and planning content to decide whether the brand feels expert, calm and trustworthy enough to design a private itinerary.

## Product Purpose

Luxury Travel Next is a public luxury travel frontend for a headless WordPress CMS. The site converts inspiration into qualified travel enquiries through editorial destination content, polished itinerary pages, trust signals and bespoke trip planning CTAs.

## Brand Personality

Editorial, composed, expert and warm. The interface should feel like a private travel atelier: refined enough for luxury buyers, practical enough to help them make route decisions, and calm enough to reduce planning stress.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS cards, cluttered travel agency pages, heavy discount-led booking UI, loud gradients, cramped sidebars, gimmicky glassmorphism and over-decorated gold accents.

## Design Principles

1. Make planning feel calm: generous spacing, clear sequence and low cognitive load.
2. Show expert restraint: use gold as a signal, not decoration.
3. Let editorial content breathe: long-form pages need comfortable line length, rhythm and hierarchy.
4. Keep conversion supportive: CTAs should feel like concierge invitations, not pressure.
5. Polish small details: icons, dividers, hover states and microcopy should all reinforce the premium feel.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target accessible contrast, visible focus states, comfortable tap targets and reduced-motion compatibility. Content should remain readable on mobile, tablet and desktop without clipped text or cramped controls.
